About Websico

Websico is a dynamic, student-led organization at the forefront of web development education. We guide participants from foundational web development concepts to advanced techniques, fostering a comprehensive understanding of the entire web creation process. Our curriculum combines theoretical insights with real-world projects, ensuring that members gain hands-on experience with both front-end and back-end development, service-side frameworks, APIs, and more. We focus on cultivating the technical skills needed to design, build, and optimize interactive and responsive websites.

At Websico, we provide resources through dedicated pages, such as our Resources and Projects pages. These platforms give users who are passionate about web development the opportunity to showcase their projects, collaborate with others, and gain exposure. We believe in empowering our community, and through these pages, we ensure that everyone can access valuable content and present their work to a wider audience.

Additionally, we regularly organize free events to help students gain hands-on experience and improve their web development skills. These events provide an excellent opportunity to learn from industry professionals, work on collaborative projects, and expand your network. At Websico, we aim to support continuous learning and encourage active participation in the evolving field of web development.

Through a supportive and inclusive community, we encourage collaboration, innovation, and engagement with the wider web development world.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you visit our website:

  • Personal Information: If you contact us, subscribe to newsletters, or submit any forms, we may collect your name, email address, and other contact details.
  • Log Files: Like many other websites, we collect and use data contained in log files. These may include your IP address, browser type, ISP (Internet Service Provider), referring/exit pages, date/time stamps, and possibly the number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, and gather demographic information.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience on our site. Cookies are small files stored on your computer that help us track your activity, provide a personalized experience, and analyze website usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect from you may be used for the following purposes:

We may use third-party services (such as Google AdSense) to serve ads on our site. These third parties may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your activity on this website and other websites to provide targeted ads based on your browsing habits.

For example, Google uses the DART cookie to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you wish to delete your data or request that we no longer use it, please contact us via the information below.
